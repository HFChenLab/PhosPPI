1.Motivation

Phosphorylation is widely studied type of protein Post-Tranlational Mofication(PTM), which mainly occurs at Serine(S), Threonine(T), Tyrosine(Y) of protein sequence. Phosphorylation is involved in multiple signaling pathways and biological process. Abnormal phosphorylation status on protein (Upregulated, Downregulated or Absence) could significantly influence protein function (such as Protein-Protein Interaction), which further lead to severe diseases1,2,3,4,5,6. In previous work, we constructed PTMint database5 by collecting complete experimental evidence of effect of six main PTM types (Phosphorylation, Acetylation, Methylation, Sumoylation, Ubiquitylation and Glycosylation) on PPI. Due to the limited experimental evidence of phosphorylation effect on PPI and great costs to experimentally identify new phosphorylation regulation evidence, therefore it is urgently need to develop a high-accuracy, user-friendly predictive method to help to explore the relationship among phosphorylation, PPI and diseases and develop targeted drugs. Here, based on PTMint database5, we developed a Sequence-based Machine Learning method (named PhosPPI) with high accuracy and AUC for predicting the effect of Phosphorylation on Protein-Protein Interactions.
